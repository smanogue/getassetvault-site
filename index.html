Frequently asked questions

Is Asset Vault free?

Yes. Asset Vault is free to download with unlimited items and properties. The AI features — identifying items from a photo and scanning receipts — include 10 free uses. After that, a one-time $9.99 in-app purchase unlocks unlimited AI. There is no subscription.

Is there a subscription?

No. Asset Vault is a one-time purchase. After 10 free AI uses, $9.99 unlocks unlimited AI identification and receipt scanning permanently. Every other feature is free.

Is my inventory shared with my insurance company?

No. Your inventory is yours. Asset Vault does not sell your data or share it with insurance carriers. Photos used with AI features are sent securely to our AI provider only to process that specific request, and your data is never used to train anything.

Do I need a special iPhone?

Most features work on any modern iPhone. The "Scan a room" AR feature uses LiDAR and requires an iPhone 12 Pro or newer; it is hidden automatically on devices without LiDAR.

Can I use Asset Vault for an insurance claim?

Yes. Asset Vault generates an insurance-ready PDF inventory report in one tap, with photos, values, and per-property breakdowns, so you have proof of ownership ready when you need to file a claim.

How does AI item identification work?

Photograph an item and Asset Vault suggests its name, brand, category, and an estimated value for you to confirm and edit. You can also scan a receipt to add multiple line items — names, prices, and dates — in one tap.
